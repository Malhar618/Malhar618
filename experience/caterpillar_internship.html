Simulations Internship

Caterpillar Inc., Advanced Power Products  •  May – Aug 2025

As part of the Large Power Systems team, I supported simulations engineering analysis team. My focus was on structural and thermal simulation of cooling components to ensure they met durability and safety requirements.

Highlights

  • Performed finite element analyses on radiator cores and fan shrouds. Used NX to build geometry and NASTRAN to solve for stresses and temperature gradients.
  • Migrated legacy models from ABAQUS to NASTRAN, along the way finding shortcomings in NASTRAN, paving the way in saving the group thousands of dollars in licensing fees.
  • Conducted a lifting study on 800 kW generator modules, evaluating rigging points, reading and diagnosing FORTRAN code, evaluating NASTRAN errors through documentation, and publishing safe handling procedures.
  • Authored documentation and training materials to onboard new engineers to NX and simulation best practices.

Skills & Tools

  • NX for CAD and meshing
  • NASTRAN for linear and thermal analysis
  • Excel VBA & Python for data processing
  • Technical writing and presentation
