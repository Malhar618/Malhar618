Three‑DOF Thrust Stand

Research Project  •  2024 – 2025

Inspired by my fellowship work, I built a smaller version of the three‑degree‑of‑freedom test stand to investigate how propulsion systems interact with control algorithms. The stand measures forces along roll, pitch and yaw axes, enabling me to validate control laws before flying on a real drone.

Key Features

  • Sturdy aluminium frame with low friction bearings
  • Force sensors calibrated for each axis
  • Data acquisition using a microcontroller and Python scripts
  • Supports rapid prototyping of control algorithms
