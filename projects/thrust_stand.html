Three‑DOF Thrust Stand

Research Project  •  2024 – 2025

During my time working on the 3DOF thrust stand, I had a couple of side projects in order to make the thrust stand physically work. With the efforts of the team I was able to optimize the thrust stand for weight ,rebuild it, and work on multiple aspects of the drone. I was able to charecterize the thrust produced by the Motor, ESC, and Propellor combination through a make shift mini thrust stand where through a MATLAB script we could derive a 7th order polynomial to feed in the custom Algorithm for the controls system inner loop. Apart from that I was able to help re-assemble the drone to rig it to the thrust stand for the tuning of the controls system.

Key Features

  • Sturdy aluminium frame with low friction slip rings and MX106T motors
  • Pixhawk based telemetry and Positioning Data
  • Data acquisition using DYNAMIXAL SDK, and acceleration through derived filter
  • Supports rapid prototyping of control algorithms
